01.About
Pokémon are fictional creatures that inhabit the world within the well known Pokemon franchise. They battle, evolve, and build bonds with their trainers, and each one belongs to one or two of 18 types—like Fire, Dragon or Dark —that shape their abilities, where they might live and the moves that can be used in battle.
Interestingly, Pokémon types aren’t random—they’re inspired by the world around us. Types like Dragon and Fairy are rooted in myths and folklore, while Steel and Electric reflect advancements in technology. Even how types interact—like Water being strong against Fire—mirrors natural relationships, blending science, culture, and creativity in fascinating ways.
